SNU developer log day 256

Today, I did further improvements to the programming section of the site, adding in a few favicons today, and updating the pages to get them to the new standard for the section. I added in support for the B programming language today.

The B programming language is archaic, so you might be wondering why I am adding it in? I want to support all programming languages on the site, even the oldest and most unsupported ones, as B has barely been used for the past 40+ years.

I updated the favicon site archive as well, and did general documentation updates. I am getting a good system in place for the site for the future structuring of the site, and the rolling release cycle that comes after the Alpha and Beta phases.

Sean Patrick Myrick on October 13th 2019
